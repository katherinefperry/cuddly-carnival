About

get to know me.

Hi! I'm Kati. Interests in data, urban design, and geography have led me down many paths, from mapmaking in local government to editing for a tech start-up (jump to my personal site for more on that). From September 2016 to June 2017, I will embark on a Fulbright English Teaching Assistantship in Spain, during which I will teach English and build on my previous research, focusing on the geography of food and food security.

My path to food was shaped not only by my profound appreciation for homemade pasta and the eternal search for the perfect bowl of gumbo, but also by my deeply held belief that every human being should eat well and with dignity.

In the coming months, I have the incredible privilege to teach in a classroom of high school students, to live vicariously through them as they compete in Model United Nations (the high school me is thrilled), and to serve in food kitchens throughout Madrid.
